Case Studies

Real-world results from Diotech engagements.

Fortune 500 Datacenter Migration

Challenge: Legacy three-tier architecture with 2,400+ VLANs, 45-minute failover times.

Solution: Spine-leaf EVPN-VXLAN fabric with automated provisioning.

Result: 99.999% uptime. Failover under 3 seconds. 60% ops reduction.

Healthcare Zero Trust Implementation

Challenge: Flat Layer 2 hospital network, no segmentation between medical devices and clinical systems.

Solution: Cisco ISE microsegmentation, NAC profiling for 12,000+ devices, zero-trust zones.

Result: HIPAA compliant. 94% lateral attack surface reduction. Zero incidents in 18 months.

Multi-Cloud SD-WAN Transformation

Challenge: 180 branches on legacy MPLS, $2.4M annual WAN spend.

Solution: Hybrid SD-WAN with dual Internet + reduced MPLS, AWS Transit Gateway, Azure vWAN.

Result: $960K annual savings. 3x SaaS performance. 14-week deployment.

SOC Build-Out for Financial Services

Challenge: No centralized monitoring, 72-hour average threat detection.

Solution: Cloud-native SOC with SIEM/SOAR, custom detection rules, open-source tooling.

Result: Detection reduced to 12 minutes. Passed SOC 2 Type II first attempt. 90% Tier 1 automation.

About Aurelio Dioval, CTO & Founder

Aurelio Dioval is a Chief Technology Officer, cloud architect, and network engineer with more than fifteen years of hands-on experience designing, deploying, and troubleshooting enterprise networks across AWS, Azure, and multi-cloud environments. He currently leads cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, AI, and enterprise networking solutions at Diotech Corp.

Previously, Aurelio served as a Senior Cloud Network Engineer III at Microsoft in Redmond, Washington, providing Tier 3+ support for enterprise customers with the most complex network challenges in Azure and hybrid environments. His career includes engineering roles at Evotec Biologics, XIOLOGIX, Lighthouse, Mindtree, EchoStar, The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas, and Smart Connections.
